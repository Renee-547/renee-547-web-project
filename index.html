Welcome to Bug Explorer

This website focuses on one of the coolest insects in the world: the tiger beetle. It is a powerful predator with incredible speed and vision.

Tiger beetles are known for their many colors and high speeds.

Why Tiger Beetles Are Interesting

  • They are among the fastest insects on Earth.
  • They have excellent vision.
  • They are active predatory hunters.
  • They hcome in a variety of colors and patterns.
